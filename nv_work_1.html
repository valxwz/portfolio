In middle mile operations, the label on a shipment shows its final destination. The core of this problem lies in the mismatch between how the system believes shipments should travel and how they should move in reality. The system calculates the path based on the shortest travel time to the final destination and assigns shipments to trips accordingly.

    Design Solution

    The team aligned on one solution-highlight the shipment destination when users need to judge whether a shipment should be onboarded to the trip-based on the following research findings:

  • In reality, regional trip planners consider a mix of shortest travel time, fewest transit hubs, and lowest van usage‚Äîfavoring efficiency and cost over speed alone.
